General information about the app

The purpose of this app is to store information about some long-term tasks and things that you don't easily remember. This is the place where you can quickly check for example when you had your hepatitis vaccination last time or when you bought new tires for the car. In fact, we think that only your imagination is the limit of what you can store in the app.

Buttons

  • Show all saved things
    • Shows you all the active things that you have saved. You can get there by clicking the blue button on the home screen.
  • Create a new thing
    • Opens a form where you can save a new thing.
  • Sign out
    • You can exit the application from the upper right corner by pressing the red "Sign out" button.
